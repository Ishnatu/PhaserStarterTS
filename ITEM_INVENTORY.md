## Enhancement System

All weapons and armor can be enhanced from **+0 to +9** using the Blacksmith's Forging System:

### Enhancement Bonuses
- **Weapons**: +1 damage per enhancement level
- **Armor**: +1% damage reduction OR +1 evasion per enhancement level (varies by piece)
- **Durability**: +10 durability per enhancement level

### Color Coding
- **White**: Base item (+0)
- **Green**: +1 to +3
- **Blue**: +4 to +6
- **Purple**: +7 to +8
- **Red**: +9 (Maximum)
- **Golden Yellow**: Shiny variant (rare, immune to destruction)

### Shiny System
- Chance to create shiny variant during successful enhancement: 0.5%-1.75% (tier-based)
- Shiny items display with golden nameplates
- Immune to destruction on failed enhancement (can still be downgraded)
- Provides prestige and aspirational goals

## Equipment Slots

Players have **8 equipment slots**:
1. **Main Hand** (weapons or shields)
2. **Off Hand** (weapons or shields)
3. **Head** (helmets)
4. **Chest** (armor)
5. **Legs** (leg armor)
6. **Boots** (footwear)
7. **Shoulders** (pauldrons)
8. **Cape** (cloaks)

### Dual-Wielding
- Players can equip two one-handed weapons simultaneously
- Two-handed weapons occupy both Main Hand and Off Hand slots
